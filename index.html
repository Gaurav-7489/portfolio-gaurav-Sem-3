Schooling

I completed my schooling at Saraswati Vidya Mandir, Banjar, with a focus on the medical stream. During these years, I was recognized for my creativity and painting skills, and I was known as an active and bright student among my peers. Alongside my main subjects, I developed an early interest in computers, which gave me the foundation to explore technology. The combination of creativity, discipline, and exposure to computers during my schooling has shaped the way I approach learning and problem-solving today.

2024 - Study Abroad Attempt

I completed the IELTS (from Rajasthan) and aimed for an immersive Software Engineering program at the University of Limerick, Ireland. Unfortunately, my medical background led to rejection, and I had to abort my plan of studying abroad. I then decided to continue my education and career journey in India itself.

2025 - College Journey

I chose NextGen CTPL because of their updated curriculum and emphasis on modern technologies. From the first day, we started learning coding and practical skills. I am currently in my 2nd year, maintaining an overall SGPA of 8.7, and continuously updating my learning journey through projects and hands-on experience.

Present & Future Plan

I used to focus on one thing at a time, but now I realize the importance of learning multiple technologies simultaneously. I am strengthening my skills in AI and Machine Learning as my main focus, while also learning Web Development, Python backend programming, Linux, SQL, and JavaScript.
